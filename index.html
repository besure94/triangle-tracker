Triangle Tracker

Input three numbers for the sides of a triangle to determine if it is an equilateral, isoceles, or scalene triangle.

Results

Equilateral

All 3 sides of the triangle are equal.

Isosceles

2 sides of the triangle are equal.

Scalene

No sides of the triangle are equal.

Not a triangle

The sum of the lengths of any two sides of the triangle is less than or equal to the length of the third side.
